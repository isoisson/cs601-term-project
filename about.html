About Me

Summary

Hi there! My name is Irene and I am thrilled to share a bit about myself. I believe in the power of connecting through words and stories so I'd like to warmly welcome you to my personal website. With a background in Business Management, I've worked in a variety of industries. In my prior role, I was a Program Manager for complex software development programs where I developed a passion for Computers and problem solving which led me towards pursuing a Master of Science in Software Development (MSSD). I am on a journey to expand my technical skills and make a positive impact for women in the tech industry.

Academic Background

My undergraduate degree was in Business Management. After 10 years of working in the Aerospace and Defense industry, I graduated with my Master of Business Administration. Following that, I was a Program Manager managing complex software programs, it was here that I developed my passion for software development. As a result, I have taken several graduate courses to deepen my technical expertise while pursuing my MSSD. I am a lifelong learner and am always eager to acquire new skills and tackle exciting challenges. In fact, I am already exploring degrees for my next academic pursuit!

  • CS526 Data Structures and Algorithms
  • CS622 Advanced Programming Techniques
