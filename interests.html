Interests

I love exploring the world

Aside from my professional and academic life, I love to travel around the world to explore new cultures. Traveling is my ultimate passion and it shapes my perspective. Exploring new places, cultures, and cuisines is my favorite way to spend time outside of work. Whether it's wandering the charming streets of a European city, savoring exotic street food in Southeast Asia, or simply gazing at the stars in a remote desert, each journey leaves an indelible mark on my heart and memories that I carry with me. Travel is a never-ending lesson and a constant reminder of the immense beauty and diversity that this world has to offer.

"Travel makes one modest, you see what a tiny place you occupy in the world." - Gustave Flaubert

To simplify things, here is a list of my most recent trips and some of my favorite things to do while I was there. Please note that cities in bold denote my favorite places:

Location Favorite things to do
Paris, France Visit the Eiffel Tower and enjoy stunning city views
Rome, Italy Eat lots of incredible Italian pasta and food
Kyoto, Japan Experience the beauty of traditional Japanese culture in the Gion District
New York City, USA Walk across the Brooklyn Bridge for panoramic views of Manhattan
Geneva, Switzerland Ski and enjoy truffle fondue
Cape Town, South Africa Drive along the stunning Chapman's Peak Drive
Sydney, Australia Climb the Sydney Harbour Bridge for incredible harbor view
Santorini, Greece Explore the ancient ruins of Akrotiri
Bangkok, Thailand Savor the delicious street food at the Chatuchak Weekend Market
Rio de Janeiro, Brazil Christ the Redeemer statue on Corcovado Mountain
